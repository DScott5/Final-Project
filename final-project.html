Coke Zero Sugar

Coke has done a phenomenal job improving the taste of Coke Zero,
making it much more palatable. The harsh zero calorie taste from the original formula
has been completely removed, giving a much more crisp finish. This new forumla helps
give the feeling of drinking regular coke while still containing zero calories.
Rating: 8/10

Snapple Fruit Punch

Snapple has brought us an interesting take on the classic beverage.
Surprisingly this was very good, and never felt too sweet because it's not
sweetened with corn syrup, just regular old sugar.
Rating: 5/10

Strawberry Bubly

I'm interested to see what this new brand of seltzer has to offer since
I'm quite the seltzer connoisseur. This particular flavor has a very fresh from the field
strawberry taste that I've never tasted in carbonated form which was very impressive.
Rating: 6/10

Vitamin Water Breeze-E

This beverage was a real doozy for me. I could hardly taste the watermelon
and had a tough time picking out the strawberry. It's like it wasn't even there to begin with.
Rating: 3/10

Canada Dry Lemonade

Canada Dry Lemonade is a very ambitious take on the ginger ale.
It's quite good but the ginger ale slightly overpowers the lemonade
making you have to really hunt for the flavor while you sip it.
Rating: 7/10

Overall Coke Zero Sugar blew all these drinks out of the water for me. It was the first drink I tried out of the bunch and the revision to the formula was very much needed in my eyes.

The weakest drink for me was the Vitamin Water Breeze-e because the taste was very faint and hardly even there.
